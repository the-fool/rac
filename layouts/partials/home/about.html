RAC was formed in 2013 as a response to a growing local interest in mixed-use spaces. Since its conception, RAC has attracted talented emerging and mid-career artists locally and nationally. The historic Richmond Hotel, converted into inspiring live-work areas, serves as a conduit for creative exchange. The list of disciplines includes digital, audiovisual, and mixed-media, which converge to create authentic experiences. We are also home to the Laboratory Spokane Residency program, which sponsors interactive work from around the world.
